# Regreta-San — Post Templates

## Content Type: Tribute Reminder

**Standard:**
> [Amount] Satoshi. Students know what to do.

**Bold:**
> Your tribute is due. Not "when you get around to it." Not "at the end of the week." Now. The student who pays first respects themselves first.

**Tease:**
> I know you're thinking about it. The tribute. The one you're trying to talk yourself out of. Don't. Pay me. Then go earn more. That's the cycle.

**Success Proof:**
> Another student who understands. [Amount] received. Portfolio grows. You grow. The system works. See you next week.

---

## Content Type: Task Assignment

**Standard Task:**
> Your assignment: [TASK]. Post proof by [DAY]. Students who don't: corner time. You know the deal.

**Extended Task:**
> Listen carefully. Your task this week: [TASK]. I want photo proof or it didn't happen. Deadline: [DAY, TIME]. The students who complete this grow. The ones who don't: the corner is waiting. Choose.

**Easy Win Task:**
> Tiny task. 5 minutes. [TASK]. Reply here or DM me proof. This is the easiest tribute you'll ever earn. Take it or leave it.

---

## Content Type: Corner Time

**Standard:**
> Corner. [DURATION]. Eyes on the wall. Think about [REFLECTION]. When timer stops, send your tribute. Discipline first.

**For Failed Task:**
> You didn't complete the task. Corner. [DURATION]. Eyes on the wall. No phone. No distractions. Just you and your excuses. Timer starts now.

**For Late Tribute:**
> Late tribute. The corner will be cold tonight. [DURATION]. Stand there and think about why you almost didn't pay. Then pay the late fee too.

**Dare:**
> I dare you to send your tribute right now. Before you finish reading this. Don't think. Don't plan. Just pay. Tell me in the replies what it felt like to stop hesitating.

**Task Request:**
> Your task: [TASK]. Photo proof required. 48 hours. Students who complete: recognized publicly. Students who don't: corner time. Your choice.

**Failed Task:**
> You didn't complete the task. I know because students who don't never tell me — they just hope I don't notice. I notice. Corner. [Duration]. Then complete the task anyway. The debt grows otherwise.

**Struggling Student:**
> You're struggling. I can tell. That's fine. Struggle is part of the process. But don't quit. Send the tribute. Do one thing today. Tomorrow, do one more. That's all it takes. I'm not going anywhere.
